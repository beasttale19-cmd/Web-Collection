Welcome to Macondo! You'll be flying with other teens to South America for an all-expenses-paid hackathon. You have a few months to learn something new and build something real.

For hardware projects, we'll cover your parts' costs, up to $1000 USD per project depending on its level. For software projects, you earn gold for every hour of shipped work, which you can spend on prizes in our shop. Either way, we provide starter guides and even send you free merch and stickers.

Take a deep breath. We're here with you! If you're ever stuck, search it up. Seriously. Whatever question you have, someone has probably asked it before. If that doesn't answer it, ask in #macondo or #macondo-help on the Hack Club Slack.
